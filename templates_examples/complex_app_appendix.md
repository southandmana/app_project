# Example Complex App Appendix

This appendix provides tips and considerations for building complex, large-scale applications (e.g., social networks, marketplaces, SaaS platforms).

## Key Considerations
- Modular architecture (microservices, monorepos)
- Scalability and performance
- Security and privacy
- Team structure and communication
- Automated testing and CI/CD
- Monitoring and observability
- Cost management
